Approach

                

                
 My UX/UI design approach takes users from frustrated to fantastic! It's all
                    about empathy first: user research (interviews, testing) helps me understand their pain points.
                    Then, I use competitive analysis to see what works and brainstorm "aha!" solutions. Information
                    architecture creates a clear user journey, while wireframing and prototyping bring ideas to life for
                    testing and refinement. Finally, visual design adds the polish for a "wow!" experience. This
                    data-driven approach ensures user-centered solutions that are both beautiful and functional.
